What we do

Alidez is building Mexico’s first cactus-based livestock feed supply chain, connecting producers, ranchers, and distributors.

Cactus pear pads are a naturally hydrating, mineral-rich feed that supports digestion and reduces reliance on costly conventional inputs. We integrate it into livestock systems to improve animal health, lower feed costs, and increase overall productivity.
