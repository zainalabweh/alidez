About CactusCoLab

Cactus Biotechnology for the World’s Drylands

We're pioneering climate-resilient agriculture through cactus bioeconomy innovation and dryland transformation

Transforming Drylands Through Innovation

CactusCoLab is building a drylands bioeconomy powered by nopal cactus. We connect producers, processing infrastructure, and markets to unlock the full potential of this resilient cactus as a source of food, livestock feed, renewable energy inputs, and bio-based products. In doing so, we turn a drought resistent desert resource into green livelihoods and climate-resilient agricultural systems.

Our Collaborators

We collaborate with research institutions, farming cooperatives, government agencies, and private enterprises across 18 countries. Together, we're building a thriving cactus bioeconomy that benefits smallholder farmers, protects ecosystems, and adapts to climate change.

Get Involved

Science-Driven Innovation

Our research combines traditional farming knowledge with cutting-edge agricultural science to develop cactus varieties optimized for climate resilience and productivity in dryland regions.

Farmer-Centered Approach

We partner directly with farming communities, ensuring solutions address real challenges and deliver tangible benefits. Our success is measured by farmer adoption and improved livelihoods.

Market-Driven Solutions

We connect producers to premium markets for cactus products, creating economic value that sustains agricultural transformation and rewards conservation efforts.
